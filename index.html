Visualization of Software Engineering Activity

In my research for the Measuring Software Engineering Report I heard of issues surrounding metrics like Lines Of Code, where
counting some aspect of software doesn't indicate the quality of what is being counted, and is not an effective measure of productivity.
This inspired me to experiment with methods to assess commit quality, and to bias evaluation in favour of higher quality commits, such
that their use in evaluating productivity is more effective. In many cases, the vast majority of contributions come from a small few developers,
and the rest of the names in the chart legend hardly have a visible impact in the chart.

For this project, I focused on 3 aspects of commits, and assigned a score to each developer in a GitHub repository based on their commit history.
The contribution of each developer relative to each other is visualised using Chart.js
